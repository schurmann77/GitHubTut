About Me

My name is Chris, and I've recently graduated from the University of Mississippi with a Bachelor's of Applied Science, Paralegal Studies. I have always held an interest in coding, but I didn't want my degree to be in Computer Science. I felt that I could get the skills I needed to learn and apply them elsewhere, and wrap in my unique way of thinking along the process.

I found a job right out of college, and they were actually a mix of Software Development and information systems, within the Real Estate realm. That job fostered my curiosity about coding even further. I taught myself the fundamentals of C#, and then went on to learn HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

Past Experiences

Like I mentioned above, I worked for a Software Development company within the Real Estate realm right out of college. It was nice, and I loved the work environment, every one was so friendly! I was looking for something more, though, something to challenge me. So I started branching out and applying to entry level developer jobs.

That's when I was given the chance to prove myself to Genesis10. They had a program called Dev10 that was absolutely perfect for someone like me. They wanted people who had graduated with a STEM degree (which mine is still a science, so it counts) but didn't require any coding experience. I decided to give it a shot, and gave their rigorous application process a shot.
